## 2. Target Tasks with Success Definitions (FL-02 through FL-04)

### Target Task 1: Code Optimization and Environment Debugging (Task 8)
* **What "Done Well" Means:** Transitioning from long, unoptimized notebook blocks to modular Python code or functional pipelines (like Docker/local environments) with zero syntax warnings, less than 2 minutes of manual debugging per environment failure, and absolute mathematical clarity on variable bounds.

### Target Task 2: Technical Content Generation (Task 9)
* **What "Done Well" Means:** Producing highly technical, engaging data science posts with 100% factual engineering terminology (e.g., explaining LightGBM DART or XAI accurately) that maintain a natural community-manager tone, requiring fewer than 3 semantic prompt corrections during the drafting loop.

### Target Task 3: Academic Essay Structuring for IELTS (Task 11)
* **What "Done Well" Means:** Elevating a raw essay draft to match IELTS Band 7.5+ criteria in "Lexical Resource" and "Grammatical Range" within 10 minutes of analysis, producing explicit tracking of alternative vocabulary without diluting the author's original argument style.
